[general]
name=Topology Split
qgisMinimumVersion=4.0
description=Self-node a line layer: extend dangles, split at all crossings and touches.
about=Adds a Processing algorithm that rebuilds the topology of a single line layer.
    It splits both lines at every crossing (X) and where a line's end touches another
    line (T), can extend dangling ends along their direction up to a tolerance until
    they meet another line, and outputs single-part LineStrings that preserve the
    original shape and attributes. Built for QGIS 4.0 (Qt6 / PyQt6).
